Chicken Rice

Description

Chicken Rice is my favourite food in Malaysia. There has been a discourse in the region on who between Malaysia and Singapore has the better chicken rice. Regardless, the factors in creating the perfect chicken rice are the rice, the chili sauce and of course, the chicken.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Chili Paste

Steps

  1. Boil chicken
  2. Cook rice
  3. Pour soy sauce on chicken
  4. Serve chicken rice with chili sauce
