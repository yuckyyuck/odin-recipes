Cendol

Description

A good cendol is hard to find but I know the secret to creating the best cendol in the world

Ingredients

  • Cendol
  • Finely shaved ice
  • Coconut milk
  • Palm sugar

Steps

  1. Put cendol into a bowl
  2. Shave ice into the bowl with cendol
  3. Pour palm sugar syrup and coconut milk into the shaved ice
