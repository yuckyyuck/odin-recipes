Nasi Lemak

Description

Looking at the photo above already makes me salivate. There's a reason why nasi lemak is Malaysia's national dish.

Ingredients

  • Peanuts
  • Fried anchovies
  • Rice
  • Coconut milk
  • Sambal
  • Cucumber slices
  • Boiled egg

Steps

  1. Cook rice in coconut milk
  2. Assemble all other ingredients on a round plate
  3. Put rice in the middle of the place surrounded by the other ingredients
